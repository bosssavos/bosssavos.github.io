About Us

Hi! We are UBISOFT.

Ubisoft is a French video game company that creates, publishes and distributes video games. It was founded in 1986 and has become one of the largest video game companies in the world. With headquarters in Montreuil, France, it has operations in over 50 countries and is known for creating popular franchises such as "Assassin's Creed", "Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six", "Far Cry", "Just Dance", and "Prince of Persia".
